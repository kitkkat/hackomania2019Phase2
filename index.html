Our Past HackOMania

"We are getting bigger!"

2018 - Creating Smart Homes

A bunch of cool geeks gathered on 24th and 25th February 2018 to combine their brain power for 24 hours, delivering deep tech, AI, and IoT solutions that impact everyday living at home through HackOMania 2018.

Despite having only 24 hours, the participants managed to come up with innovative projects by integrating AI solutions with smart locks, Raspberry Pi, Arduino boards, and paper cardboards.

The winning team walked away with $1500 cash prize!

2017 - Social Networks

On the 4th to 5th of March 2017, geeks congregated and utilized APIs from 4 major social networks to develop meaningful applications.

Laptop crashes, API-side failures, and sleep deprivation didn't stop the teams from changing the world with code in each of their small ways back in HackOMania 2017.

In the end, a combined prize pool of $900 was awarded to the winning teams.

2016 - Smart Nation

Geeks got together for HackOMania 2016: a 24-hour hackathon from 18-19 March 2016 aimed at building meaningful projects that are inline with the Smart Nation theme that Singapore is promoting.

Over the course of a full day, contestants harnessed open-source government APIs via data.gov.sg to develop brilliant and impactful applications.

For HackOMania 2016, a grand total of $900 worth of prizes were given away to our eventual winners.
